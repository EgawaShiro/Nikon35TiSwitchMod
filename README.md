# Nikon35TiSwitchMod
Want to modify your Nikon 35Ti 's push button to later switch version. Here is a solution.
You will need to 3D print the files: "Nikon 35Ti Switch Toggle" and "Nikon 35Ti Switch".
You will need to cut and bend a 0.125 mm thick stainless steel/copper shim stock into the shape guided by "Nikon 35Ti Switch Contact". This part is then glued to the 3D-printed "Nikon 35Ti Switch Toggle" according to the "Nikon 35Ti Switch Assem" instruction. 
You are expected to carefully grind the leading edge as well as the rear edge of the 3D-printed "Nikon 35Ti Switch Toggle" so that it can slide freely inside the "Nikon 35Ti Switch" part. 
You are also expected to prepare a micro spring "0.15x1x3mm". This specific model is avaliable in TaoBao. Trim this spring to 2 mm for reduced spring compression and less switch toggle force.
You will need to prepare an SS 1 mm bearing ball.
For painting, I used Durabake firearm oven-cured enamel. The paint is diluted by Toluene at 1:3 ratio and applied by an airbrush, followed by curing at 300F non-ventilative oven for 30 mins.
